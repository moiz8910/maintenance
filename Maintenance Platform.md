# Maintenance Platform

Application will have 7 main sections:

1. Performance Management
2. Inventory Status
3. Asset Information
4. Work Management
5. Resource Management
6. Safety
7. Maintenance Agents

## 1. Performance Management

This section will have KPIs as per user persona + access to a "Talk to data" agent.

## 2. Inventory Status

* It will show in real time status of MRO items
* It will highlight the MRO items nearing (within 5%) or below safety stock as well as those that are significant above safety stock or at risk of obsolescence

## 3. Asset Information

* It will provide a user-friendly way for the user to navigate the asset hierarchy. For each asset (subject to access), the user can access all the information corresponding to an asset (including work order history, KPIs, relevant documents such as SoP, OEM documents etc., sensor data and CBM/PdM alerts)

## 4. Work management

* User can see the status of all current work orders including drilling down into individual ones

## 5. Resource management

Enables the user to see the status of all the human resources available including:
* Inhouse resources
* T&M resources
* Third party contracts (e.g. Fixed, Lump Sump)

## 6. Safety

* Safety incidents
* Safety reports

## 7. Maintenance Agents

* Asset Data Steward
* Dynamic asset strategy agent
* Maintenance autopilot
* Diagnostic agent
* Intelligent Business Analyst & Advisor
* Incident & Near-miss analyst
* Digital work instruction coach
* Reliability Assistant
